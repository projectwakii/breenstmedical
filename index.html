After Hours Service

Breen Street Medical Practice offer an after hours service for patients outside of normal business hours through the National Home Doctor Service during weekdays between 6.00pm to 8.00am, Saturdays from midday all day Sunday and Public holidays. To access this service phone 137425. For calls outside of these times please call the Practice on 5444 0689 to be redirected.

Make An Appointment

Please phone (03) 5444 0689 during business hours for an appointment. We will make every effort to accommodate your preferred time and GP.

If you feel that you may require a long consultation, please let staff know and they will be happy to book a double appointment. Informing reception at the time of booking can avoid lengthy delays. (All patients are able to obtain an appointment within two working days).

Emergencies are always given priority.
